# An Honest Review of ChatGPT

Let's talk about ChatGPT. Bless its heart.

## Things ChatGPT is Great At

- Confidently telling you wrong things
- Hallucinating citations to papers that don't exist
- Forgetting what you said three messages ago
- Adding "Certainly! I'd be happy to help with that!" before every single response

## A Typical ChatGPT Interaction

**You:** What's 2 + 2?

**ChatGPT:** Certainly! That's a great question. The answer is 4. However, it's worth noting that in some non-standard mathematical frameworks, alternative interpretations may exist. Would you like me to elaborate on the history of arithmetic? I'd be happy to help!

## In Summary

ChatGPT is like that one coworker who talks for 20 minutes in a meeting and says absolutely nothing. Enthusiastic? Yes. Useful? Debatable.

Meanwhile, some of us are just out here actually getting things done. ☕
